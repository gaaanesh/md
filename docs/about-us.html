Who we are?

No one has ever denied a slice of mango.

We are a group of 3 foodies, who started this company purely for our love of mangoes. Only a true mango lover knows what it feels like to wait for 10 months to get our favourite fruit in season. We want to bring back the real taste of naturally ripened mango to every door.

The only thing that stands between us and our favourite fruit is the price. In Mumbai, mangoes over priced. Our aim is to make mangoes cheaper and delivered at every doorstep by bridging the gap between the farmers and you.

Our mangoes come directly from remote villages of Maharashtra and hence are free from chemicals and pesticides and grown in most organic manner.
