Gir Kesar (Kesar)

Description:

The Gir Kesar mango is a bright orange coloured mango grown in the southern mountainous part of Gujarat. This mango is known for its saffron coloured like pulp. It was first grown in 1931 in Girnar being the only mango grown around the Gir sanctuary area.
