Who we are?

Mango Dolly is founded by three engineers originating from the rurals of Maharashtra. As true lovers of mango, we used to visit our Ratnagiri every summer, just to savour the rich sweet taste of freshly ripened mangoes.

Our goal is to free our customers from the hassle of tiring themselves in the scorching heat looking for handpicked best mangoes at reasonable rates. Only a true mango lover knows what it feels like to wait for 10 months to get our favourite fruit in season. We want to bring back the real taste of naturally ripened mango to every door.

The only thing that stands between us and our favourite fruit is the price. In Mumbai, mangoes are over priced. Our aim is to make mangoes cheaper and delivered at every doorstep by bridging the gap between the farmers and you.

Our mangoes come directly from remote villages of Maharashtra and hence are free from chemicals and pesticides and grown in most organic manner.
