Pairi

Description

Pairi mangoes are not typically very sweet, they have a sour bite. With a reddish tinge on its skin, it works the best for an alternative to Kesari.

Keeping quality is not great so consume them quickly post purchase. This is one of the varieties of mangoes to hit the market early in the season. Known to be fibrous and juicy, it is one of the most popular choices for the popular aam ras in the western states of India.

Weight: 150 - 250 grams

Origin: Madhya Pradesh
